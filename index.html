#2. A #WORD, FROM SIMEON DAVID, OUR #FOUNDER:

Being a medical student was not really a favourable experience for me, as I experienced a lot of psychological stress. Going through what I went through, made me realize and felt, a great necessity, for addressing and putting more emphasis, to the concept of mental and emotional health.

Other aspects of medicine, included in the company, sprouted from my realized passion for medicine, and generally, human health; I have a subtle affection for children, and a profound sense of gratitude, (developed overtime), for certain elders and youngins, who helped and encouraged me, during my dark and trying times.

The second aspect of my tertiary educational pursuit, began when I left the medical profession; Computer Science. In the course of studying Computer Science, I realized the possibility, of merging Medicine, and Technology; popularly referred, (mostly on social media), as HeathMeetsTech, (usually as a hashtag); liveraging on technology to complement problem solving processes, that relates to medicine, and in this case, as a company, extends to other fields like entrepreneurship; I related this idea to my medical and technological friends, who found it valuable, and hence made it possible, for us to be here.

It gives me great joy - when I see people experience and express, their TRUE and ACTUAL, sense of happiness, and joy - and sadness, when I hear of suicide victims, AND/or, people who don't ACTUALLY, experience that TRUE and ACTUAL, sense of happiness, and joy, or good mental AND/or, emotional health; hence, iMex MediTech Company.

I look forward to seeing this company become a world-class company, that helps people solve their problems, and empowers them to lead a great and happy life.
